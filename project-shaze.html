Luxury Product Design

"A sculptural barware collection designed to reinterpret tea and cocktail rituals through contemporary form, material expression, and refined user interaction — aligned with Shazé's design philosophy."

Design Direction

FORM & PHILOSOPHY

The design intent was to reinterpret cocktail rituals as expressive experiences by transforming functional serving objects into bold, sculptural statement pieces. The design balances contemporary form with craftsmanship, creating objects that feel refined, tactile, and visually distinctive while remaining purposeful in use.

  • Form language — geometric structure softened by fluid, organic transitions
  • Material — solid brass, chosen for tactile richness, durability, sculptural presence
  • Polished for vibrancy, antiqued for grounded sophistication
  • Ergonomic designs that enhance user experience without compromising form
  • Visual impact — pieces designed to feel like jewels of the bar

Form Language

Geometric meets organic — bold lines softened with fluid curves, creating visual tension.

Materiality

100% brass — polished for vibrancy, antiqued for grounded sophistication.

Functionality

Ergonomic designs that enhance user experience without compromising sculptural form.

Visual Impact

Pieces designed to feel like jewels of the bar — collectible, distinctive, exquisite.

Moodboard — material, form and luxury direction

Ideation

CONCEPT EXPLORATION

Ideation explored multiple object typologies within barware — bottle openers, bottle stoppers, cocktail stirrers, strainers, and shakers. Each was explored in both hand-drawn sketch and digital 3D form to evaluate sculptural potential.

  • Bottle openers — key form, compass form, shell circular form
  • Bottle stoppers — splash form, anchor, artefact-inspired
  • Stirrers & bar spoons — elongated forms with decorative heads
  • Strainer — circular disc with handle, ornamental perforations
  • Final collection — cohesive family of objects sharing geometric-organic vocabulary

Ideation sketches — exploring bottle openers, stoppers, and stirrer forms

Final Product

The final outcome is a sculptural cocktail serving ensemble that transforms ritual into an experiential statement. Designed with bold geometry and refined detailing, the collection balances artistic expression with functional precision. Premium materials and meticulous craftsmanship reinforce Shazé's uncompromising quality, while contemporary forms introduce a sense of audacious individuality. Each element is thoughtfully resolved to elevate both hosting and gifting, resulting in a distinctive, high-design piece that embodies sophistication, exclusivity, and expressive luxury.
